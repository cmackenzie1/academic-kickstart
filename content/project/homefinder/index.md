Homefinder is a project in collaboration with Robin Mwesigye and CMPUT 401 at the University of Alberta. Over the course of 3 months, a team of 5 people rapidly
developed an application to help students find housing with like minded students. The project is not yet live, however when it is, you will find it [here](https://www.rentwhiz.ca/)
